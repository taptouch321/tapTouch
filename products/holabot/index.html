FAQs

Everything you need to know

How big is the collection robot?
Holabot weights up to 50kg and has a size of 534 x 542 x 1213 mm.
How fast can it transport items?
It can transport items very vast! According to the many businesses that have used Holabot and our testing scenarios, Holabot can transport items six times faster than a typical waiter can serve meals.
Is Holabot waterproof?
Yes! Holabot's inner cabin is certified to IPX5, the highest level of particle protection for electrical equipment. Waterproof utilities are rated from IPX0 to IPX5, so rest assured, no electrical damage will occur when using this unique robot!
Can I use this with both wet and dry goods, e.g., food items, paper products?
Yes! The waterproof inner cabin is easily removable and washable so that you may put in dirty plates, medical instruments, or dusty warehouse hardware.
